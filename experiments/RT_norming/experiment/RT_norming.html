Study Debrief


Thank you for completing this study! There are no more questions left to answer — feel free to click SPACE to advance to your completion code at any point.

To provide some context about the study you just participated in: we are studying the effects of racialized stereotypes on language processing, and how the societal presence of a racial stereotype can affect how we understand language.

This study focused specifically on the commonly-held racial bias that Black men are more likely to be associated with crime and criminality.[1, 2] It has been shown that these harmful stereotypes shape the way we perceive the world;3 this study applies the same ideas to how we process language.
