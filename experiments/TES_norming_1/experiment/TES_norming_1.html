Instructions


In this study, you will read snippets of stories that are unfinished. The unfinished parts of the stories are left as blanks.

When you see the snippets, you will be able to help the author complete the story! All you have to do is fill in the empty box with the word (or phrase) that would make most sense in the blank.

Each story is about a different main character, so remember to keep in mind who the story is about when filling in the blank.

The authors of these stories are interested in making them realistic and interesting, so try to write the first response that comes to mind and don't worry about using fancy or uncommon words/phrases. Also, the authors want the stories to be different, so each response should be unique and specific.
